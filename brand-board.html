08 — Brand Rules
Do's & Don'ts
Guard the brand. Every pixel matters.

✓ Do This

  • Use flat, minimalist UI with clean whitespace
  • Emerald Green for primary actions and branding
  • Burnt Gold ONLY for critical CTA buttons
  • High-quality photos of real human creators
  • Thin-line professional iconography (1.5px stroke)
  • Right-to-left layout as default for Arabic
  • Inter/Outfit (EN) + IBM Plex Arabic (AR) fonts
  • Focus messaging on "Product" and "Profit"

✗ Don't Do This

  • No neon colors, AI-generic purple/cyan palettes
  • No heavy glassmorphism or excessive gradients
  • No cartoonish or bulky icons
  • No stock photography of smiling models
  • No font weights above 900 or below 300
  • No rainbow gradients on text or buttons
  • No feature-focused messaging (talk profit)
  • No generic "Web3" or "crypto bro" aesthetics
